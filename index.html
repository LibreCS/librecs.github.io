Developing Cutting-Edge Software for New Hardware Technologies

At LibreCS, we are committed to the development of open-source software tools and systems that drive the boundaries of technology. Our focus is on enhancing features, performance, and reliability in both professional and enthusiast applications. Our innovative projects cater to low-resource environments and non-standard process architectures, delivering industry-grade solutions across diverse hardware.

  • plcd: A lightweight program designed to remotely monitor and log the uptime of Programmable Logic Controllers (PLCs). It offers uptime detection, monitoring, and logging along with automatic port sniffing and controller detection.
  • vqgarm: An implementation of VQGAN + CLIP for testing arm64 processors.
  • nlmate: Our latest project, a powerful open-source tool for scraping, preprocessing, and labeling datasets from specified Wikipedia pages. It is particularly useful for training Natural Language Processing (NLP) models.

All of our software is licensed under the GNU GPLv3, and we encourage its use in other open-source projects.

At LibreCS, we strive to ensure that software is open and avaliable for development, implementation, and iteration. We invite you to further delve into the world of cutting-edge software and hardware technologies by exploring our repository of publications and contributing to our open-source projects.

  • Read Articles: We regularly publish insightful articles on our website. Expand your knowledge and keep up with the latest trends in technology on our publications page.
  • Contribute to Projects: We welcome and value contributions from the open-source community. You can contribute to our projects on GitHub and help us in driving the boundaries of technology with our source code avaliable on our GitHub page.
